Welcome International Students!


Complete your application here, after which you DO NOT need to contact/apply separately to any college. Let us handle your admission process till a college has been allocated to you, while you sit aback awaiting your final admission letter.

Here’s your single window admission process guide      



Step 1 : Register (as foreign national if you do not hold and Indian Passport,else as NRI/PIO/OCI/whose  parent works in gulf or south-east Asia) and login.

Step 2 : Explore colleges that offer your desired course / Apply to your preferred college.

Step 3 : Click on Add to wish list and complete your profile Information first (if pending),without which you will not be able to see the course in wish list.

[If you have any queries regarding transcripts, you can refer to the documents required section under important information or go to query section and ask a question to us]

Step 4 : Select minimum 5 (maximum 10) colleges, subject to availability,from the given list only(that offer offer your desired course) in the order of your preference.

[ In case you know any college that offers the course and don’t find it in the list, you can send us an email at uom@admissiondesk.org, from your registered email ID, with the name of colleges and the course]

Step 5 : Proceed for payment of eligibility fees (Rs.3863/course)
For example : If a student applies to 10 colleges for B.Com and another 10 colleges for BMS,then he will have to download
two separate challans and make two separate payments (Rs.3863 x 2courses)
THIS FEE IS NON REFUNDABLE.

Step 6 : Click on Online Payment, fill details and complete the payment. You will receive an acknowledgement/receipt of this payment, if successful.

Step 7 : Go to MY APPLICATIONS section and await your provisional admission letter. Takes about 10-20 days. Once approved by the department, you will see Accepted and will be able to download the letter from there itself. Keep tracking receipt of the same.

Step 8 : In case you have any queries / are facing any problems, go to the Query section and ask a Question(Preferably attach a screenshot of the error). We shall revert


Step 1 :  Go to MY APPLICATIONS and Complete the application process, step by step.

Step 2 :  Download the provisional admission letter / eligibility letter

Step 3 :  Upload your Student Visa (Unless  you're a student of Nepal/Bhutan or holding Indian Passport)

Step 4 :  Download your Application Letter

Step 5 :  Make payment towards application fees($1150) using a debit/credit card or by Net Banking and upload the Acknowledgement/receipt of your successful transaction.
THIS FEE IS NON REFUNDABLE.

Step 6 :  You will receive enrolment details. Now wait for verification of your payment and allocation of a college.

Step 7 :  Once college has been allocated, make online payment of college fee(third payment). Click on Proceed for payment to view your college fee amount. After successful payment, download the challan/receipt and upload the same there itself.

Step 8 :  You will be able to download a Firm letter(which you have to present to the college Registrar)

Step 9 :  Visit Health care centre in University of Mumbai, kalina campus and get your medical check-up

Step 10: Upload your Medical Certificate, RP copy and EPT passing details. (where RP = Residential Permit& EPT = English proficiency test)

ALL INFORMATION IS SUBJECT TO CHANGE AS PER UNIVERSITY GUIDELINES.
