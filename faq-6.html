FRRO and AIU

FRRO

What is FRRO ?

FRRO stands for "Foreign Regional Registration Office".It is a special central police branch that will issue .Log on to their website for more detaisl : https://indianfrro.gov.in/eservices/

Does every student have to visit FRRO

Not all students need to register themselves with FRRO.Only foreign Nationals/citizens of a country other than India(excluding : students holding PIO or OCI card/NRI students /students from Nepal & Bhutan) are required to register with FRRO department in India within 14 days of arrival in India.

What is FORM S ?

It is the STUDENT FORM provided by your respective colleges for FRRO purposes(or by University of Mumbai, if you're studying in it's department).

What is FORM C ?

It is the RESIDENTIAL FORM that mentions the place of stay in india. It is issued, along with the rent agreement, to the tenant by the landlord whether it's a hostel or paying guest or a friend/relative's house.

Where is FRRO located?

"Address for FRRO Mumbai is : 78/1, Badruddin Tayabji Marg, behind st.xevier collage, Dhobi Talao, Chhatrapati Shivaji Terminus Area Fort Mumbai, Maharashtra-400001"

How to reach FRRO?

You could use public transport( railway - CST station and a cab thereafter) or private transport like ola/uber etc.We advise you to put the address on google maps before your visit.

Is there any concerned person we can meet at FRRO ?

Authorities may keep changing, so we cannot help you with one name.However, you maybe guided on your arrival at their mentioned address.

What are the documents required to be submitted

Kindly visit their website for detailed information (https://indianfrro.gov.in/eservices)

How much money do we have to pay to FRRO ?

FRRO does not charge any fee to students.However, if you visit them after the time limit of 14 days, they shall charge you with a late fee.

What is the procedure at FRRO ?

You have to create an account online and furnish information online.Kindly visit their website for detailed information (https://indianfrro.gov.in/eservices)

AIU

What is AIU ?

Association of Indian Universities (AIU) is an organisation and association of major universities in India. It evaluates the courses, syllabi, standards, and credits of foreign Universities pursued abroad and equates them in relation to various courses offered by Indian Universities.The AIU is mainly concerned with the recognition of Degrees/Diplomas awarded abroad for the purpose of admission to higher degree courses in Indian Universities.

What is AIU Equivalence certificte ?

Students who come to India for studies need to obtain Equivalence Certificate issued by the Association of Indian Universities (AIU), New Delhi.The Evaluation Division of AIU issues the Equivalence Certificate to students with foreign qualifications to facilitate their admission in Indian Universities.Through the AIU Equivalence certificate, they certify that the student’s education is equivalent to the education required as per Indian system of education for admission to Indian Universities.Thus if the elgibility department requires a student to submit the equivalence certificate, then the student must apply for it.

What are the Eligibility Conditions for grant of equivalence certificate?

Equivalence is granted to students if he/she meets the following eligibility conditions:

  1. The degree has been awarded by a university which is duly approved/recognized/accredited in its own country;
  2. The student has pursued the programme of studies as a full time regular student on the campus of the university in the foreign country;
  3. The minimum eligibility qualification for admission to the programme and duration of the programme of the study is at least the same as prescribed in India;

what are the Documents Required to apply for AIU equivalence certificate ?

  3. Copies of the academic certificate of education.

What is the fees for applying to AIU?

AIU is a separate governmental body and has no connection with the University of Mumbai, which is why also, the fees has to be directly paid by the student to AIU and they will provide a receipt for the same along with the AIU equivalence certificate.Kindly look up their website for further details : www.aiu.ac.in.
