What documents are required for bachelors/masters/Ph.D course?

  • Required documents depend majorly on the registered category.*click here* to download a document with detailed explanation for each category. However in case you're applying for a masters course, you shall also be required to submit details upto your bachelor's degree/ under grad & graduation.
  • We do not offer admissions for PhD course as yet.

I'm unable to upload a transcript

First check should be for the file size and acceptable format.If you're still unable to upload a particular trancript, we expect the scanned copy to have become a corrupted file.Kindly re-click a photograph of or re-scan the transcript and try to upload again.Also ensure it's not a password protected file.If none of this is the case, kindly raise a ticket from help section/send us an email at uom@admissiondesk.org with subject - TRANSCRIPT from your registered e-mail ID

I have not yet received my marksheet (or any other transcript)

We do understand that a few boards release final results a little later than the rest.In this case we suggest our students to upload their pre-board/ preliminary/equivalent results temporarily and apply for eligibility.However, College allocation shall be done based on final marksheet only, so ensure that you upload your final marksheet as soon as it is received by you.In any other case, admission shall be subject to cancellation.
