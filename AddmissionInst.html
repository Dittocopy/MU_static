Important Admission Instructions


1: Fees charged for International Student is five times the fees of Indian Student

2: Provisional admission is not a guarantee for Final admission.

3: Registration Fees (Eligibility + Application) is non-refundable.

4: Students who are particular about specific college for UNDERGRADUATE course should come well in advance.

5: The University Departments have the credit system and all classes begin in July/August. So students who desire admission to the University department should report to the International Center at Mumbai University in July or before 31st July.

6: This instruction is for International Students who have passed the qualifying examination from outside of India and require fresh "Students Visa" for admission to various general courses and courses in the Faculties of Management and Pharmaceutical Science.For such students, the admissions have been centralized and it is the office of the Director, International Center, which will issue the letter of Admission provisional as well as firm, as per the procedure and the rules laid down in this regard. Fresh InternationalStudents are required to pay Registration fees in addition to other Fee (Application form and Eligibility fees).

7: For Postgraduate and Research degree (M.Phil./ Ph.D. Courses) in the faculties other than Medicine, Engineering, Management and Pharmaceutical Sciences: Admissions to all the postgraduate courses being offered in the P.G. Centers have been centralized & admission letters to International Students shall be issued by the Office of the Director, International Center. In other words, Principals of affiliated colleges where PG centers are located shall not grant admission to International students directly. An International student wishing to undertake M.Phil./ Ph.D. or any other research degree at Mumbai University or any other affiliated college or institute recognized byMumbai University, MUST have prior security clearance from the Ministry of Human Resource Development, New Delhi and MUST BE on "RESEARCH" visa endorsed by Mumbai University only

8: TRANSFERS Once an international student has taken admission in a particular College/ Institution he/ she shall not be allowed to change the College/ Institution if the name of that College/ Institution is endorsed/mentioned in the students Visa. In other words, he/ she shall be required to study in the College/ Institution which is mentioned in the students Visa. In special circumstances, an International student seeking a transfer from one college to another must apply to the International Centre for such a transfer with NO OBJECTION LETTER from the Principal of the College where he was originally admitted and an ACCEPTANCE LETTER from the Principal of the college where he wishes to join. The student can join the new college only after he/ she is issued a TRANSFER LETTER by the International Centre. Fees charged is: Click here for fees details.

9 Admission of International Students other than FRESH and MIGRATING International Students. All International Students who are already registered under Mumbai University and want to continue their next degree under Mumbai University will have to pay fees. Click here for fees details.

10: International Students who are not registered with the International Centre shall not be permitted to appear at the University examinations.

11: See instructions for English Proficiency Test (EPT) : Click here for EPT details.

12: The student must always read the Notices/ circulars put up on the NOTICE BOARD of the college/ Department/ Institution concerned and the University.

16 : Police Registration: The student should Register in Police Station within 14 days of his/her arrival failing which he will be fined.
17 : The student should note that his admission stands automatically cancelled if :

a. He/ She fails to obtain long term Student/ Research Visa in the name of Mumbai University only from the IndianEmbassy in his/ her country on the basis of the letter of provisional admission.
b. He/ She fails to produce Original Statement of marks/ Grade point average certificate of the last qualifying examination at the time of admission.
c. He/ She fails to appear and pass the proficiency test in English, entrance and other tests where-ever prescribed.
d. He/ She is found to be medically unfit.
e. He/ She fails to fulfill any condition of eligibility.
